Fried Rice

Description

Chinese fried rice is the quintessential comfort food. Think of itâ€”a bowl of steaming white rice cooked to just the right consistency, filled with bits of meat and vegetable. No wonder fried rice is one of the world's most popular rice dishes!

Ingredients

  • Cooked,chilled rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots, onions, green onions, and peas
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce and Oyster sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs using butter, then set aside
  2. Sautee vegetables and garlic
  3. Add rice and sauces to pan along with butter and stir fry
  4. Remove pan from heat and stir in eggs, sesame oil, and green onions
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Serve
