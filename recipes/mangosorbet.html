Mango Sorbet

Description

Mango, lime, sugar. These ingredients are just destined to be together in a sorbet! A perfect way to cool down on a hot day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 3 ripe mangoes
  • 3 tbspn lime juice

Steps

  1. Heat sugar, salt and water in pot to make simple syrup
  2. Prep mango by separating mango flesh from seeds and skin, rough chop
  3. Blend mango, simple syrup, and lime juice in blender
  4. Chill blended mixture in fridge
  5. Process in ice cream maker
  6. Freeze until firm
