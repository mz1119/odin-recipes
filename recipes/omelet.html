Omelet

Description

Not only is an omelette quick and easy to make, it is a paragon of economy. Odds and ends (a.k.a. leftovers) rise to a new level when placed inside an omelette.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • Bacon
  • Cheddar
  • Spinach
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Beat eggs lightly with a fork
  2. Melt butter in a nonstick pan
  3. Add eggs into pan and let sit for a minute
  4. Use a spatula and tilt the pan to allow uncooked eggs to get cooked
  5. Add fillings
  6. Fold and serve
